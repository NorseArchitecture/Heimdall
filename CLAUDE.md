## 1. What This Repository Is

Heimdall is **the gate** — `Norse.AuthN`: the authn story built on Himinbjörg, presenting login, register, forgot-password, 2FA setup, recovery, and reset uniformly across Blazor Server, WASM, and MAUI, plus the backing gRPC service. It is the topmost realm in the dependency chain among the current submodules — nothing else rides above it.

`AuthN.Components`, `AuthN.Components.FluentUI`, and `AuthN.Services` are live — the Himinbjörg→Heimdall component migration moved the injection-clean subset (Login, Register, Logout, and their validators/requests) over mechanically; components with real backend injections (`UserManager`/`SignInManager`/`HttpContext`) exist only on Himinbjörg's unmerged `feature/identity-web-server` branch, pending the gRPC wireup slice. `AuthN.Services` was hoisted out of `AuthN.Components` (2026-07-24) to isolate `IAuthenticationService` (the gRPC contract) and its wire records (`LoginRequest`, `RegisterRequest`, `LogoutRequest`, `LoginResult`) from Razor/FluentValidation/Blazilla — a consumer who wants only the gRPC declarations, wrapped in their own components and wired to whatever backend they choose, references this thin assembly and nothing else. `IAuthenticationGateway`/`AuthenticationResult` (the Blazor-facing gateway abstraction, produced by a host-specific implementation) stayed behind in `AuthN.Components`, unaffected. **Not yet done:** Himinbjörg's `Identity.Web.Server` and Yggdrasil's `Hosting.Web.Client`/`Hosting.Web.Server`/`Hosting.Stories.Client` still resolve these types off a single `using Norse.AuthN.Components;` — since Bifröst wires the platform via `UseProjectReferences` for local dev, those repos won't build until their `using` statements are updated to add `Norse.AuthN.Services`; that follow-up was deliberately deferred to keep this hoist scoped to Heimdall. Pages still carry the ASP.NET Identity scaffold's `/Account/*` routes deliberately — route renaming is a separate, deferred curation pass, not bundled into relocation.
